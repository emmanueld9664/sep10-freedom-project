Explanation of the Website
This website is the culmination of all my work for this years Freedom Project that I decided to make on Music. For context, it is important to know about the music filter. In which people who listen to their established likes in genre will only listen to that genre and nothing else. With this website I will show you how I plan to fix this problem. Through all my research, I have found a few solutions and came up with my own.
Solutions Found While Researching - Unordered
  • Using Spotify, most notably its mobile feature that mixes in songs you didn’t even have in your playlist.
  • Listen to the radio to find new songs you may like.
  • Research the type of mood the genre emphasizes.
  • Record Labels.
  • Asking others for their opinions.

The Ordered List of My Solutions
  1. Add a set on or off function to get rid of the autoplay if it annoys the user. Spotify itself doesn’t even have an option for this.
  2. Add a randomized playlist function. This function will allow users to choose if they would like to listen to completely new songs or genres.
  3. Give users the option to select from new genres every time they enter the website. While it may not seem big, this feature will help those willing to experiment with their taste in music.
  4. The advanced option: Have an A.I. automatically generate music, similarly to how Jukebox OpenAI extends music with A.I. More of a choice that can be done extremely well with future technology.
Out of all these choices, I decided to go with option 2, as I feel it is something within my range of skill.
